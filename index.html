Who We are

Skyline Racing Academy
it is a professional Academy Racing team which provides services to it’s client. Skyline was formed by professional drivers and champions. Skyline provide coaching to kids and adults with different genders on two stroke e.g. Rotax engines. Skyline focus on training the basis of driving go Kart from race line to competition level and basic mechanic level. Our vision to create a place that graduate experienced drivers to be the best in the field with our experience and mechanics we can build a competitive community to represent us and our country in the best manner
